# Communication & Messaging

4 use cases for automating communication and messaging with OpenClaw.

## Use Cases

### Email & Communication Automation (Featured)
**Difficulty:** Beginner | **Popularity:** 3

Complete email delegation, customer follow-ups, and automated messaging across platforms. Handles inbox management and professional correspondence.

**Key capabilities:**
- Inbox zero automation
- Email drafting and responses
- Customer follow-up sequences
- Multi-platform messaging
- Professional correspondence management

---

### Chat Platforms & Bot Development
**Difficulty:** Intermediate | **Popularity:** 2

Interactive Discord bots, Telegram automation, and rich messaging interfaces with custom UI components.

**Key capabilities:**
- Discord bot creation and management
- Telegram automation workflows
- Custom UI components in chat
- Multi-platform bot deployment
- Interactive messaging experiences

---

### ElevenLabs Voice Conversation
**Difficulty:** Intermediate | **Popularity:** 2

Natural back-and-forth voice conversations using ElevenLabs for realistic speech synthesis. Send voice notes and receive spoken responses.

**Key capabilities:**
- Natural voice conversations
- ElevenLabs integration
- Voice note sending and receiving
- Realistic speech synthesis
- Conversational AI interactions

---

### Beeper Unified Messaging
**Difficulty:** Intermediate | **Popularity:** 1

Monitor and respond to messages across all platforms through Beeper. WhatsApp, Signal, Telegram, Discord, and more - all in one chat interface.

**Key capabilities:**
- Unified inbox for all messengers
- WhatsApp, Signal, Telegram support
- Discord integration
- Cross-platform responses
- Message routing and prioritization
